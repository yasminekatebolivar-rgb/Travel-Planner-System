About the System

The Barangay Resident Record Management System is a digital platform designed to help barangay staff efficiently record, organize, and manage resident and household information. It replaces paper forms and spreadsheets with a centralized and structured database.


👤 Intended Use: Single-user / Admin-only (authorized barangay personnel). Functions include adding, updating, searching, and reporting resident data to support barangay operations and planning.

Overview

This system streamlines record management by digitizing the process, allowing barangay staff to maintain accurate and updated resident data. It also enables faster reporting, easier tracking of demographics, and better community planning insights.

Key Features

🧾 Resident Information

Add, view, update, and manage complete resident records efficiently.

🏠 Household Profiling

Group residents by household, identify family heads, and track members.

🔎 Search & Filter

Find residents easily by name, age, or address with quick filtering options.

🏷️ Categorization

Tag residents as Senior Citizen, PWD, Student, Voter/Non-voter, etc.

📊 Reports & Statistics

Generate population reports, household counts, and age distributions.

🔒 Admin Access

Restricted system access for authorized barangay personnel only.
