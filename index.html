Overview

The Barangay Resident Record Management System (BRRMS) is designed to streamline the process of recording, updating, and managing resident and household data. By replacing manual methods, the system improves data accuracy, accessibility, and efficiency in barangay operations.

About the System

BRRMS empowers barangay personnel to maintain a centralized, secure, and easily accessible database of resident profiles. It ensures that all records are up to date, helping local officials make informed decisions and generate accurate reports for planning and governance.

Intended Users: Authorized barangay staff only.

Purpose: To digitize record-keeping, reduce errors, and support transparent barangay management.
