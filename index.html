What is BarangayHub?

BarangayHub is a comprehensive Resident Record Management System designed specifically for barangay staff and administrators. It revolutionizes how communities organize and manage resident information.

This intelligent platform replaces traditional paper-based systems and scattered spreadsheets with a centralized, secure digital database. Your data is always accessible, organized, and protected.

Enterprise-grade data protection

Why Choose BarangayHub?

Managing resident records shouldn't be complicated. Our system streamlines every aspect of record management, from data entry to generating comprehensive reports.

Authorized barangay personnel can effortlessly add, update, search, and generate reports on resident data—all while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection.
