About the System

The Barangay Resident Record Management System (BRRMS) is a digital platform designed to help barangay staff efficiently record, organize, and manage resident and household information.

This system replaces traditional paper forms and manual spreadsheets with a centralized and secure database to ensure data accuracy, accessibility, and confidentiality.

Authorized barangay personnel can add, update, search, and generate reports on resident data‚Äîsupporting local governance, population monitoring, and planning initiatives.

  • Reduces paperwork and redundancy
  • Enables faster certificate processing and reporting
  • Enhances transparency and accountability
